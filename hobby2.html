✿ Sketching ✿

In the field of sketching I am still a beginner, however, I find joy in every stroke and take delight in the simple yet magical act of bringing images to life on paper.
Even in the vast domain of sketching , I particularly connect with cartooning.
I believe cartooning allows me to express the playful and childlike side of my personality.The vibrant colours, exaggerated features and whimsical characters provide a creative outlet for self- expression.
It always amazes me how one can express complex narratives through cartooning in a such a simple manner.
Sometimes it also becomes a way for me to escape the reality and becomes my another source of joy.
To me, sketching has a therapeutic value. The act of drawing is a meditative process ,offering me a way to relax, relieve stress and channel emotions into a creative and positive endeavour.
Sketching is like my personal canvas where imagination meets expression, creating endless possibilities with every stroke.
